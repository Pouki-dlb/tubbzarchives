About

What is TUBBZ®?

TUBBZ® is a collectibles brand specialising in officially licensed cosplaying rubber ducks inspired by globally recognised franchises across film, television, gaming, and entertainment. Each figure reimagines iconic characters as highly detailed, humorous duck collectibles, appealing to fans, collectors, and pop culture enthusiasts. The brand combines novelty design with licensing partnerships to create a distinctive product category within the collectibles market.

Why this project?

  • An exhaustive archive of every TUBBZ® duck ever released. Many ducks are only on sale for a limited time — once a figure sells out, the product page disappears from the brand's website. That makes this heritage hard to preserve: if you arrive too late, you can miss real gems. This project aims to maintain the most complete inventory possible.
  • A collection tracker. For the brand's biggest fans with an ever-growing duck collection, it’s a way to follow your progress and mark what you already own, so you never lose track.

Save your progress

Your progress (the TUBBZ® you own, your wishlist and notes) is stored only in this browser. There is no account and nothing is sent anywhere.

That means your data can be lost if you clear your browsing data, use a different browser, or switch to another device.

To keep it safe, use Export to download a backup file, and Import to restore it later or on another device. Exporting from time to time is the best way to avoid losing your progress.
