Save your collection

Your collection (the versions you own, your wishlist and notes) is stored only in this browser. There is no account and nothing is sent anywhere.

That means your data can be lost if you clear your browsing data, use a different browser, or switch to another device.

To keep it safe, use Export to download a backup file, and Import to restore it later or on another device. Exporting from time to time is the best way to avoid losing your progress.
